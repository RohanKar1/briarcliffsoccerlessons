About Us

Meet the coaches.

Rohan Karnik

Founder & Trainer

My name is Rohan Karnik. I have played soccer for 10 + years, and next fall will be my third year on the Briarcliff Boys Varsity Soccer team. I have a strong passion for the game, and want to share that with others who want to learn more about the game. Playing sports has been crucial in my life, not only for athletics, but the other qualities they have taught me. My goal is to help younger kids improve and have fun playing soccer.

Gabriel Lucht

Goalkeeper Trainer

Hi, I’m a student at Briarcliff High School and the varsity goalkeeper for the soccer team. Soccer has been a big part of my life, and playing goalie has taught me leadership, focus, and how to stay calm under pressure. I’m also a strong student in school, and I try to bring the same work ethic from the classroom onto the field.
